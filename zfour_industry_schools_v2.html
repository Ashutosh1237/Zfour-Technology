The Challenge

Why Schools Struggle with Traditional HR

Every industry has unique HR pain points. Here are the ones we hear most from schools.

📚

Academic Calendar Complexity

School HR must manage attendance against academic calendars — summer vacations, exam periods, and staff training days all need different treatment.

💰

Teacher Payroll Complexity

Teaching staff payroll includes DA, HRA, medical allowances, academic incentives, and different scales for aided vs unaided institutions.

📋

Substitute Teacher Chaos

Managing substitute teacher daily payments, tracking coverage for absent staff, and maintaining qualification records manually is a constant pain.

⚖️

Aided School Compliance

Government-aided schools face additional compliance — Pay Commission revisions, audit requirements, and education department reporting.

🔄

High Non-Teaching Staff Turnover

Support staff (peons, drivers, security) have high turnover, requiring constant onboarding, PF enrollment, and exit processing.

📊

Manual Attendance Registers

Biometric installation costs and manual attendance registers make tracking teacher punctuality, late arrivals, and academic hours laborious.

→ Result: School administrators spend 20+ hours monthly on HR paperwork that should be automated

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best HRMS for schools in India?›
The best school HRMS handles academic calendar-based attendance, teaching and non-teaching payroll with appropriate allowances, educator leave policies (including summer vacation accounting), PF/ESI/TDS compliance, and gratuity management for long-serving staff.
How does Zfour handle academic calendar-based attendance?›
Zfour allows schools to configure their academic calendar including working days, vacation periods, examination schedules, and staff training days. Attendance and leave calculations automatically adjust to the academic context.
Can Zfour manage different pay scales for teaching and non-teaching staff?›
Yes. Zfour supports separate pay structures for teaching staff (with DA, HRA, academic allowances) and non-teaching staff (support staff wages), each with their own compliance and reporting requirements.
Does Zfour handle government-aided school compliance?›
Yes. Zfour can be configured for Pay Commission salary structures, government audit requirements, education department reporting formats, and the specific compliance requirements of aided educational institutions.
How does Zfour calculate summer vacation leave for teachers?›
Zfour leave policy engine can be configured with vacation period rules where teacher leave during summer vacations is tracked separately from earned leave, ensuring accurate leave balances throughout the academic year.
Can Zfour track substitute teacher payments?›
Yes. Zfour supports daily/hourly payment processing for substitute teachers, tracks which classes were covered, and maintains a substitute teacher database with qualification records for quick deployment.
How does Zfour handle teacher gratuity calculations?›
Zfour auto-calculates gratuity liability for each employee based on years of service and last drawn salary. Schools can see current gratuity provisions and process retirement or separation gratuity payments accurately.
Is Zfour suitable for a small school with 20 staff?›
Yes. Zfour works for institutions from 10 to 2,000+ staff members. A 20-person school benefits immediately from automated payroll, leave management, and compliance filing — without the complexity of enterprise systems.
