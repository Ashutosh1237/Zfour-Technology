What is HRMS Software? — The Complete Answer

HRMS (Human Resource Management System) is a cloud platform that centralises and automates your entire HR function — from hiring and onboarding to payroll, attendance, leave, compliance, and offboarding — in one integrated system.

  • Replaces disconnected spreadsheets with one intelligent system for all HR workflows
  • Ensures 100% compliance with Indian statutory rules — PF, ESI, TDS, PT, Gratuity, and Bonus Act
  • Provides real-time visibility into attendance, payroll costs, headcount, and workforce analytics
  • Empowers employees with self-service access to payslips, leave balances, and HR data on mobile

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before choosing Zfour HRMS.

What is the best HRMS software in India for SMEs?▲
Zfour HRMS is purpose-built for Indian SMEs with native PF/ESI/TDS compliance, GPS attendance, shift management, and complete payroll automation — starting at ₹99/employee/month. It combines all HR workflows in one integrated platform.
How much does HRMS software cost in India?▼
Zfour starts at ₹99/employee/month (Starter) and ₹149/employee/month (Growth, includes GPS and shift management). Enterprise pricing is custom for 500+ employees. All plans include setup support, GST invoice, no hidden charges.
How long does HRMS implementation take?▼
Most customers go live within 7 working days. Our team handles data migration, configuration, and training. Enterprise organisations typically take 2–3 weeks with a dedicated implementation manager.
Does Zfour handle Indian statutory compliance automatically?▼
Yes. PF (12% + 13.61%), ESI (0.75% + 3.25%), Professional Tax (state-wise), TDS Section 192, Gratuity, LWF, and Bonus Act — calculated every month automatically. All statutory returns (PF ECR, ESI returns, Form 24Q) generated one-click.
Is there a mobile app for employees?▼
Yes. iOS and Android apps available. Employees can clock in/out with GPS, apply for leave, view payslips, check leave balances, and raise HR queries — all from their phone. Works on low-bandwidth connections for manufacturing and field use.
Can we migrate from Keka, GreytHR, or Zoho People?▼
Yes. Zfour supports data migration from all major Indian HR platforms. Our implementation team handles migration of employee master data, historical payroll records, attendance history, and opening leave balances. Data migration is included in all plans.
Is my employee data safe on Zfour?▼
All data is stored on Indian servers (AWS Mumbai region) and never leaves India. 256-bit AES encryption, role-based access controls, two-factor authentication, and regular security audits. Compliant with India's IT Act 2000 and PDPB framework.
