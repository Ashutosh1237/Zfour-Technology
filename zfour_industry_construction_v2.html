The Challenge

Why Construction Struggle with Traditional HR

Every industry has unique HR pain points. Here are the ones we hear most from construction.

📍

Multi-Site Attendance Chaos

Tracking 500+ workers across 5 construction sites with paper registers leads to ghost workers, attendance fraud, and inflated wage bills.

💸

Daily-Wage Payroll Complexity

Calculating daily wages, overtime at 2×, site allowances, and skill-based differentials for hundreds of workers manually invites errors.

👷

Sub-Contractor Compliance Risk

Principal employers are liable for sub-contractor compliance failures. Without visibility, you inherit their PF/ESI defaults and face penalties.

🏗️

BOCW Act Non-Compliance

Missing Building & Construction Workers Act registrations, welfare fund contributions, and worker benefit entitlements lead to project shutdowns.

🔄

Workforce Volatility

Construction worker attendance varies daily based on weather, skill availability, and project phase. Manual tracking is impossible at scale.

📋

Labour Contractor Paperwork

Managing Form-5, Form-13, CLRA licenses, and principal employer compliance certificates for each sub-contractor is a full-time job.

→ Result: Construction companies lose 5–8% of project revenue to wage fraud, compliance penalties, and workforce inefficiency

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best HRMS for construction companies in India?›
The best HRMS for Indian construction companies needs GPS attendance for remote sites, daily-wage payroll automation, sub-contractor compliance monitoring, BOCW Act management, and CLRA license tracking. Zfour HRMS is purpose-built for this complexity.
How does GPS attendance work for construction sites?›
Workers install the Zfour mobile app and mark attendance within a GPS geo-fence set around each site. Supervisors can also mark attendance for groups. The system flags anyone outside the geo-fence, eliminating proxy attendance.
Does Zfour handle daily-wage payroll for construction workers?›
Yes. Zfour calculates daily wages based on actual attendance, applies overtime at 2× the basic rate, adds skill-based differentials, and processes weekly or fortnightly wage disbursement with complete PF/ESI compliance.
How does Zfour monitor sub-contractor compliance?›
Zfour tracks each sub-contractor's PF challans, ESI payments, CLRA license status, and required form submissions. Principal employers get automated alerts when a contractor is at risk of default — protecting you from inherited liability.
What is BOCW Act compliance and how does Zfour handle it?›
The Building & Construction Workers Act requires worker registration, BOCW cess payment (typically 1% of construction cost), and welfare benefit entitlements. Zfour automates worker registration tracking, cess calculation, and welfare fund management.
Can Zfour track workers across multiple project sites?›
Yes. Zfour provides a multi-site dashboard showing real-time attendance and headcount for all active projects. Each site can have separate supervisors and attendance rules while HR gets a consolidated view.
How does Zfour handle migrant worker compliance?›
Zfour tracks inter-state migrant worker documentation under the Inter-State Migrant Workmen Act — registration, travel allowances, and welfare fund contributions — with state-wise compliance requirements managed separately.
Does Zfour support offline attendance for remote construction sites?›
Yes. The Zfour mobile app supports offline attendance marking that syncs when connectivity is restored — critical for remote construction sites with poor internet coverage.
